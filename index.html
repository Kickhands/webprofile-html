About Me

Based in Indonesia, I’m passionate about building minimal yet expressive digital products. My approach blends strategic thinking, code craftsmanship, and an eye for aesthetics inspired by brutalist layouts. When I’m not at the keyboard, I’m exploring coffee shops, photography, or sketching new ideas.

I also dive deep into data analysis & engineering—wrangling SQL datasets, shaping Power BI dashboards, building Excel automations, and scripting Python pipelines that inform decisions long before a UI ships.
